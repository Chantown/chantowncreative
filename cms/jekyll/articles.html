Articles

Vickie Chan is a freelance writer and copywriter with experience covering various topics including lifestyle, arts and culture and travel as well as more business oriented topics like sustainability and digital. An active and experienced writer, Chan is able to write in a number of styles, including first person pieces, for social content and for news.

Her articles have appeared in SCMP (Post Magazine, Lifestyle, 48 Hrs, Education Post), Gafencu magazine, Asia Weekly, Tatler Macau and more.
